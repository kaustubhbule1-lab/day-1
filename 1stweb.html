Virat Kohli

About

Virat Kohli is a famous Indian cricketer. He is known as one of the best batsmen in the world.

Personal Information

Full Name: Virat Kohli

Date of Birth: 5 November 1988

Country: India

Role: Batsman

Career

Virat Kohli has played for the Indian cricket team in all formats like Test, ODI, and T20. He has scored many runs and centuries in international cricket.

Achievements

  • Former captain of Indian cricket team
  • Won many international awards
  • One of the highest run scorers in cricket
